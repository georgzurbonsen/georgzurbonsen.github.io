B4P is a Low-Code Solution for data analysts to improve their performance in extracting insights from big data. B4P is a data integration and analytics engine with built-in programming language, and is purpose-built for integration and analysis of large complex datasets, rapidily generating results in Excel and other business intelligence tools.
