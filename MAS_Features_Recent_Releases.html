Overview

The roots of B4P date back to 2007 when the first C/C++ object class library has been developed to process tables, but the solutions were still coded in C/C++. The first version of the B4P language appeared in 2013, following a series of improvments and extensions aiming to solve any form of business and analytical problems involving big data.
